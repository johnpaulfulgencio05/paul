Here are some of our top-rated Filipino dishes:

Adobo

A savory and tangy Filipino dish made with chicken or pork marinated in vinegar and soy sauce.

Sinigang

A tamarind-based soup with various vegetables and your choice of meat, known for its sour flavor.

Kare-Kare

A rich Filipino oxtail stew with vegetables, served with a thick peanut sauce.

Lechon

A popular Filipino dish featuring roasted pig with crispy skin and tender meat.

Sisig

Sisig is a sizzling dish made from chopped pig's face and ears, usually served on a hot plate.

Shanghai

Shanghai rolls are Filipino spring rolls filled with ground pork and vegetables.

Pancit

Pancit is a term for Filipino noodle dishes, often stir-fried with a variety of ingredients.

Bulalo

Bulalo is a hearty Filipino beef soup made with bone marrow and vegetables.
